JATSPack is ...

... an extension of EXPath packaging

So you can also include libraries in XSLT, XQuery, XProc, XML Schema, RelaxNG, Schematron, and NVDL.

EXPath packaging is already supported by several tools, including: the eXist and Qizx XML databases, the Saxon XSLT and XQuery processor, and the Calabash XProc processor. (Support in Saxon and Calabash is via third-party plugins.)
