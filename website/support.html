Support / contact information

There are many ways to get help or to contact the group.

Feel free to create a topic in either of the two forums on SourceForge: the help forum, or open discussion forum. Or, you could send an email to the project administrator, either through the Sourceforge message form, or directly to chrismaloney (at) users.sourceforge.net.
