%contrib-info.class;

Contributor Information Class Elements

Definition

Elements that may be used to provide additional information about a Contributor, including external links such as <ext-link> and <uri>, as well as internal links such as <fn> and <xref> (For more such elements, see the Parameter Entity %contrib-model;.)

Declaration

<!ENTITY % contrib-info.class
                        "address | aff | author-comment | bio |
                         email |  etal | ext-link | fn |
                         on-behalf-of | role | uri | xref"           >
