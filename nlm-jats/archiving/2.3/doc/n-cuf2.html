Document Hierarchy Diagrams

This section contains diagrams of document structures defined by the Journal Archiving Tag Set (Green) and base Suite modules. Diagrams for the “top-most” element, Article and its major structural components (e.g., Front Matter, Body of the Article, etc.) are presented first. Thereafter, the contents of each major structure are diagrammed, followed by diagrams of structures commonly used throughout the Tag Set and base Suite such as Figure, List, and Table Wrapper. (Because these “common” structures are used in a variety of models, they are presented in alphabetical order, rather than hierarchically.) The diagrams are presented in the following order:
